Cuttlefish (n)

Cephalopod

Encyclopædia Britannica describes the cuttlefish as: any of several marine cephalopods of the order Sepioidea, related to the octopus and squid and characterized by a thick internal calcified shell called the cuttlebone.

At Cuttlefish Creative, the cuttlefish is our mascot for adaptability, embodying the process of designing and developing for a client. We won't force our vision on you. We work with you to realize yours. This requires us to be flexible and able to bring our skills and talent to your aesthetic. It means we consider multiple approaches to challenges and that we are constantly growing and learning.
